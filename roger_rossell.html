[Website] STATEMENT

Rossell's personal work has the human being at its center. The topics of his works revolve around the concepts of identity, privacy, intimacy and eroticism. Working mainly with chemical photography and interested in all the phases of the process, he has spent the last years researching and experimenting with historical printing techniques, such as cyanotype, gum bichromate and carbon transfer.
